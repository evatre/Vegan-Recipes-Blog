Tofu Curry Noodle Bowl

This is one of my favorite dishes, that i randomly discovered on the internet. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 150g Tofu
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 red onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 heads of baby bok choi

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180ºC. Bake the tofu on parchment lined sheet pans for about 20 minutes, until golden and slightly crispy.
  2. Meanwhile, sauté the cumin seeds and mustard seeds until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the red onion, garlic cloves, green chilies, turmeric, cinnamon, chili powder and curry powder. Sauté for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the coconut milk, baby bok choy and baked tofu, then Cook until cooked through and the bok choy is just wilted.
  5. Serve over brown rice noodles.
